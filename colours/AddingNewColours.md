

## Adding New Colours

Go for it! Try to keep the chroma of your color sets relatively low. Build Canada is, overall, a **serious** brand, and the best way to communicate that visually is to use **desaturated** colours.
You can indicate **complexity**, as in sophistication, by using so-called *secondary* and *tertiary* colours. 

In a digital space, **secondary** colours are midpoints between the primaries.
