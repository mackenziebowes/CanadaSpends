Adding New Colours

Go for it! Try to keep the chroma of your color sets relatively low. Build Canada is, overall, a serious brand, and the best way to communicate that visually is to use desaturated colours. You can indicate complexity, as in sophistication, by using so-called secondary and tertiary colours.

In a digital space, secondary colours are midpoints between the primaries.

Look at the following hex codes:

Red
#FF0000
Green
#00FF00
Blue
#0000FF

The secondary colours mix these together:

Yellow
#FFFF00
Cyan
#00FFFF
Magenta
#FF00FF

Tertiary colours mix primaries with secondaries:

#FFAA00
 
#FF00AA
 
#AAFF00
 
#AA00FF
 
#00FFAA
 
#00AAFF
 

And remember, you can always desaturate any colour by decreasing the distance between primaries:

#FFAAAA
 
#AAFFAA
 
#AAAAFF
